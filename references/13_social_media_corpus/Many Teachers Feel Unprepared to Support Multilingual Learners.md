# Many Teachers Feel Unprepared to Support Multilingual Learners

**Source:** https://www.rand.org/pubs/research_reports/RRA134-29.html
**Crawled:** 2026-06-17

Using national teacher and principal survey data from spring2024, the authors examine U.S. teachers' perceptions about how prepared they feel to teachmultilinguallearners (MLLs), the adequacy of their curriculum materials for addressing the needs of MLLs, and the extent to which educators prioritize supports for MLLs when selecting professional learning, instructional materials, and activities.
