## 3. Task Details

### Q1: Word Association (词汇联想 / Zeitwortassoziation / Word Association)

**Instruction (ZH)**: 请尽可能快速地写出你对「时间」一词的五个联想词
**Instruction (DE)**: Bitte schreiben Sie möglichst schnell die ersten fünf Wörter auf, die Ihnen zum Begriff „Zeit" einfallen.
**Instruction (EN)**: Please write down the first five words that come to mind when you see the word: "Time"

**Purpose**: Activate semantic field; measure cultural variation in temporal concepts.
**Scoring**: Number of unique associations; semantic distance from core concept.

---

### Q2: Untranslatable Word (不可译词汇 / Unübersetzbare Wörter / Untranslatable Word)

**Instruction (ZH)**: 请用汉语和你能够熟练使用的任意一门外语解释汉语中「孝」这个字
**Instruction (DE)**: Bitte erklären Sie das deutsche Wort „Fernweh" sowohl auf Deutsch als auch in einer Fremdsprache
**Instruction (EN)**: Please explain the English word "Privacy" in both English and a foreign language

**Key Design Choice**: Each language version uses a **culturally specific** untranslatable word:
- **Chinese**: "孝" (filial piety) — central Confucian concept with no direct Western equivalent
- **German**: "Fernweh" (wanderlust/longing for faraway places) — deeply German concept
- **English**: "Privacy" — fundamental Western concept with no direct Chinese equivalent

**Purpose**: Test conceptual transfer and cultural specificity.
**Scoring**: Conceptual overlap between L1 and L2 explanations.

---

### Q3: Emotional Reaction (情感反应 / Emotionale Reaktion / Emotional Reaction)

**Instruction**: Imagine you said something wrong in front of 20 people and everyone laughed. What would you say immediately?

**Purpose**: Elicit spontaneous, colloquial expression; test pragmatic competence.
**Scoring**: Formality level; emotional valence; cultural specificity.

---

### Q4: Picture Description — Vase (看图写话：花瓶)

**Image**: Boy knocks over a vase, vase breaks on the floor.
**Constraint**: Maximum 20 words/characters.

**Purpose**: Test event description; agent-patient assignment; causal reasoning.
**Scoring**: Completeness (agent, action, result); spatial framing.

---

### Q5: Translation — Mike Sentence (翻译：Mike 句子)

**Source**: "Mike came out of the room, walked through the living room, and went into the garden."
**Constraint**: Do not omit direction/location information.

**Purpose**: Test motion event encoding; path vs. manner encoding.
**Scoring**: Information preservation; syntactic structure.

---

### Q6: Picture Description — Spatial Positions (看图写话：空间位置)

**Image**: Pen on left of book, cup behind book.
**Constraint**: Do not omit direction/location information.

**Purpose**: Test spatial frame of reference; relative vs. absolute positioning.
**Scoring**: Spatial term usage; reference point consistency.

---

### Q7: Translation — Test Sentences (翻译：考试句子)

**Source**: "The exam is in six days." + "The exam has been moved forward by two days."
**Constraint**: Express both sentences.

**Purpose**: Test temporal reasoning; tense/aspect systems.
**Scoring**: Temporal accuracy; tense marking.

---

### Q8: Picture Description — Exchange (看图写话：交换)

**Image**: A gives a book to B.
**Constraint**: Describe the scene.

**Purpose**: Test transfer event encoding; dative construction.
**Scoring**: Agent-patient-recipient mapping; verb choice.

---

### Q9: Translation — Mother Umbrella (翻译：雨伞句子)

**Source**: "Because it was raining yesterday afternoon, the mother gave an umbrella to the girl."
**Constraint**: Do not translate word-for-word; express naturally.

**Purpose**: Test causal reasoning; information structure.
**Scoring**: Causal connective; word order; naturalness.

---

### Q10: Creative Naming — Hospital Robot (创造名称：医院机器人)

**Scenario**: Hospital robot with 7 functions (temperature, medication, vitals, wheelchairs, doctors, beds, waste).
**Constraint**: Create an official name that communicates function.

**Purpose**: Test conceptual composition; naming strategies.
**Scoring**: Informative value; brevity; cultural naming conventions.

## 5. Data Collection Protocol

### Procedure

1. Participant receives questionnaire in their **native language**
2. Participant answers all 10 tasks in their **native language**
3. For Q2, participant also explains the concept in a **foreign language**
4. Participant completes demographics section
5. Questionnaire is returned to researcher

### Consent

- Written informed consent obtained before participation
- Consent form available in ZH/DE/EN
- Minor participant protection (parental consent for <18)
- Right to withdraw at any time (GDPR Art. 7)

### Data Storage

| Data Type | Storage | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Raw responses | Encrypted, local | Destroyed after analysis |
| Anonymized data | Local, gitignored | Archived after study |
| Consent forms | Locked cabinet | 10 years (legal requirement) |
