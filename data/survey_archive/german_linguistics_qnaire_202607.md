## Note: Format Mismatch

This questionnaire is a **linguistics/cognition questionnaire** with 10 language-production tasks. It is NOT the LinguaGraph 5-topic concept extraction survey (`cognitive-space/survey/index.html`) which asks open-ended responses to: Erfolg, Freiheit, Heimat, Gerechtigkeit, Verantwortung.

These responses CANNOT be processed through the LinguaGraph concept extraction pipeline because:
- Different question format (sentence production vs. concept description)
- No concept-level responses suitable for knowledge graph extraction
- Designed for linguistic analysis, not cognitive structure analysis

**Recommendation**: Archive separately. These responses are from a different research instrument.
