IB Mathematics: Analysis and Approaches SL/HL

=== Topic 1: Number and Algebra ===

1.1 Arithmetic Sequences and Series
- nth term: aₙ = a₁ + (n-1)d
- Sum of first n terms: Sₙ = n/2 [2a₁ + (n-1)d]

1.2 Geometric Sequences and Series
- nth term: aₙ = a₁ · r^(n-1)
- Sum of first n terms: Sₙ = a₁(1-rⁿ)/(1-r)
- Infinite geometric series: S∞ = a₁/(1-r) when |r| < 1

1.3 Exponents and Logarithms
- Laws of exponents: aᵐ · aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ, (aᵐ)ⁿ = aᵐⁿ
- Logarithms: log_a(x) = y ⟺ aʸ = x
- Change of base: log_a(x) = log_b(x)/log_b(a)

1.4 Complex Numbers
- z = a + bi where i² = -1
- Modulus: |z| = √(a² + b²)
- Argument: arg(z) = arctan(b/a)
- Polar form: z = r(cos θ + i sin θ)

=== Topic 2: Functions ===

2.1 Functions and Their Graphs
- Domain, range, intercepts, symmetry
- Transformations: translations, reflections, stretches

2.2 Quadratic Functions
- f(x) = ax² + bx + c
- Vertex: x = -b/(2a)
- Discriminant: Δ = b² - 4ac

2.3 Rational Functions
- f(x) = P(x)/Q(x)
- Vertical asymptotes: Q(x) = 0
- Horizontal asymptotes: degree comparison

2.4 Exponential and Logarithmic Functions
- f(x) = aˣ, g(x) = log_a(x)
- Natural exponential: f(x) = eˣ
- Natural logarithm: g(x) = ln(x)

=== Topic 3: Trigonometry ===

3.1 Radian Measure and Circle Geometry
- Arc length: s = rθ
- Sector area: A = ½r²θ

3.2 Trigonometric Functions and Identities
- sin²x + cos²x = 1
- tan x = sin x / cos x
- Double angle formulas: sin 2x = 2 sin x cos x

3.3 Trigonometric Equations and Graphs
- General solutions for trig equations
- Amplitude, period, phase shift
