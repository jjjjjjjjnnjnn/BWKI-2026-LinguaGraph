Khan Academy Math — Kindergarten & 1st Grade

K.CC: Counting and Cardinality
- Count to 100 by ones and by tens
- Write numbers from 0 to 20
- Understand the relationship between numbers and quantities

K.OA: Operations and Algebraic Thinking
- Understand addition as putting together and adding to
- Understand subtraction as taking apart and taking from
- Add and subtract within 10

1.NBT: Number and Operations in Base Ten
- Count to 120, starting at any number
- Understand place value: tens and ones
- Add within 100 using place value

1.MD: Measurement and Data
- Measure lengths indirectly and by iterating length units
- Tell and write time in hours and halves
- Organize, represent, and interpret data

1.G: Geometry
- Distinguish between defining attributes of shapes
- Compose two-dimensional shapes or three-dimensional shapes

Khan Academy Math — 2nd Grade

2.OA: Operations and Algebraic Thinking
- Add and subtract within 20
- Work with equal groups of objects (foundation for multiplication)

2.NBT: Number and Operations in Base Ten
- Understand three-digit number as bundles of hundreds, tens, ones
- Count within 1000; skip-count by 5s, 10s, 100s
- Add and subtract within 100 using place value

2.MD: Measurement and Data
- Measure the length of an object using appropriate tools
- Tell and write time to nearest 5 minutes
- Solve problems involving dollar bills, quarters, dimes, nickels, pennies

2.G: Geometry
- Recognize and draw shapes with specified attributes
- Partition rectangles into rows and columns of same-size squares

Core chain: number → addition → subtraction → place value → measurement → shapes
