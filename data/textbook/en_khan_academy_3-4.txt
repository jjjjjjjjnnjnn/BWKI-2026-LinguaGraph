Khan Academy Math — 3rd & 4th Grade

3.OA: Operations and Algebraic Thinking
- Multiply and divide within 100
- Solve problems involving the four operations
- Identify arithmetic patterns

3.NBT: Number and Operations in Base Ten
- Use place value understanding to round whole numbers
- Fluently add and subtract within 1000

3.NF: Number and Fractions
- Understand a fraction 1/b as the quantity formed by 1 part of a whole
- Represent fractions on a number line
- Explain equivalence of fractions

3.MD: Measurement and Data
- Tell and write time to nearest minute
- Measure and estimate liquid volumes and masses
- Draw scaled picture graphs and bar graphs
- Relate area to multiplication and addition

3.G: Geometry
- Understand that shapes from different categories may share attributes
- Partition shapes into parts with equal areas

4.OA: Operations and Algebraic Thinking
- Interpret a multiplication equation as a comparison
- Solve multistep word problems
- Find all factor pairs for numbers 1-100

4.NBT: Number and Operations in Base Ten
- Generalize place value understanding for multi-digit numbers
- Fluently add and subtract multi-digit whole numbers
- Multiply a whole number of up to four digits by a one-digit number

4.NF: Number and Fractions
- Explain why a fraction a/b is equivalent to (a×n)/(b×n)
- Add fractions with like denominators
- Multiply a fraction by a whole number

4.MD: Measurement and Data
- Know relative sizes of measurement units
- Apply area and perimeter formulas

4.G: Geometry
- Draw points, lines, line segments, rays, angles
- Classify two-dimensional figures based on parallel or perpendicular lines

Core chain: multiplication → division → fractions → decimals → area/perimeter
