Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics (0580)

=== Paper 1/3: Core Curriculum ===

Topic 1: Number
- Integers, decimals, fractions
- Operations with whole numbers, decimals, fractions
- Percentage, ratio, rate
- Standard form (scientific notation)
- Significant figures and estimates
- Bounds of accuracy

Topic 2: Algebra
- Algebraic expressions and simplification
- Linear equations in one variable
- Simultaneous linear equations
- Quadratic equations (factorizing, formula)
- Inequalities (linear and quadratic)
- Sequences and patterns
- Functions and mappings

Topic 3: Coordinate Geometry
- Cartesian coordinate system
- Gradient (slope) of a line
- Equation of a straight line: y = mx + c
- Parallel and perpendicular lines
- Midpoint and distance between two points

Topic 4: Mensuration (Measurement)
- Length, area, volume formulas
- Surface area of prisms, cylinders, spheres
- Arc length and sector area
- Speed, distance, time calculations
- Density, mass, volume

Topic 5: Geometry
- Angles: properties of triangles, quadrilaterals, polygons
- Circle theorems
- Symmetry (rotational and reflective)
- Similarity and congruence
- Enlargement (dilation)

Topic 6: Trigonometry
- SOH CAH TOA
- Sine rule, cosine rule
- Area of triangle = ½ab sin C
- 3D trigonometry

Topic 7: Vectors and Transformations
- Vector notation
- Translation vectors
- Transformations: reflection, rotation, translation, enlargement

Topic 8: Probability
- Sample space, tree diagrams
- Mutually exclusive events
- Independent events
- Conditional probability

Topic 9: Statistics
- Mean, median, mode, range
- Frequency tables and diagrams
- Cumulative frequency
- Histograms

=== Paper 2/4: Extended Curriculum (additional topics) ===

Topic 10: Matrices
- Addition, subtraction, multiplication
- Determinants (2×2)
- Inverse matrices
- Solving simultaneous equations using matrices

Topic 11: Transformations with Matrices
- Reflection, rotation, translation, enlargement as matrices

Topic 12: Vectors
- Column vectors
- Vector operations
- Position vectors

Topic 13: Graphs of Functions
- Quadratic, cubic, reciprocal, exponential functions
- Intersection of graphs
- Gradients of curves (tangents)

Topic 14: kinematics
- Constant acceleration formulae
- Distance-time and velocity-time graphs

Topic 15: Sets
- Venn diagrams
- Set notation
- Union, intersection, complement

Topic 16: Further Trigonometry
- Radians
- Exact values of trig functions
- Graphs of trig functions
- Trigonometric identities

Topic 17: Further Algebra
- Indices (exponent laws)
- Logarithms
- Factor theorem
- Remainder theorem

Topic 18: Matrices (Extended)
- 3×3 matrices
- Determinants (3×3)
