Khan Academy Math — 5th & 6th Grade

5.NBT: Number and Operations in Base Ten
- Understand the place value system
- Fluently multiply multi-digit whole numbers
- Divide up to four-digit dividends by two-digit divisors
- Add, subtract, multiply, and divide decimals to hundredths

5.NF: Number and Fractions
- Add and subtract fractions with unlike denominators
- Interpret a fraction as division of numerator by denominator
- Multiply a fraction or whole number by a fraction
- Divide fractions by fractions

5.MD: Measurement and Data
- Convert among different-sized standard measurement units
- Relate volume to multiplication and addition

5.G: Geometry
- Graph points on the coordinate plane
- Classify two-dimensional figures into categories based on properties

6.RP: Ratios and Proportional Relationships
- Understand the concept of a ratio
- Use ratio reasoning to solve problems
- Compute unit rates associated with ratios of fractions

6.NS: Number System
- Fluently divide multi-digit numbers
- Fluently add, subtract, multiply, and divide multi-digit decimals
- Find the greatest common factor and least common multiple

6.EE: Expressions and Equations
- Evaluate expressions at specific values of variables
- Identify equivalent expressions
- Understand solving equations as a process of reasoning

6.G: Geometry
- Find area of triangles, trapezoids, and other polygons
- Find volume of right rectangular prisms with fractional edge lengths
- Represent 3D figures using nets

6.SP: Statistics and Probability
- Recognize a statistical question
- Summarize and describe distributions
- Understand measures of center and measures of variation

Core chain: fractions → decimals → ratios → expressions → equations → statistics
