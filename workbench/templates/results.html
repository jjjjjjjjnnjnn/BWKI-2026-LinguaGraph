Language Drift Score (LDS) — Methodology

The Language Drift Score quantifies structural divergence between knowledge graphs extracted from different languages:

LDS(A, B) = 1 − |A ∩ B| / |A ∪ B|

A value of 0 indicates identical concept structure; a value near 1 indicates maximal divergence. LDS is computed for each language pair (ZH-EN, ZH-DE, DE-EN). A human validation study is in preparation to confirm the metric.
