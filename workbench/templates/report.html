The Chinese corpus provides the most extensive coverage of fundamental mathematics (elementary and middle school), while the English and German corpora focus primarily on secondary and tertiary mathematics education.

4. Structural Observations

Cross-lingual invariance: Concepts that appear in multiple languages exhibit structurally similar neighborhoods, suggesting that the underlying mathematical knowledge structure has language-independent invariants — while pedagogical organization differs across curricula.
